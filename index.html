Who We Are

PT Timor Sea Grow We are an Indonesian agricultural exporter delivering high-quality, traceable, and sustainable commodities to global markets. Our core products include premium dried seaweed, authentic Indonesian spices, and robust Robusta coffee — cultivated with care and integrity.

Our Vision

To become a trusted bridge between Indonesian producers and world buyers — driven by quality, transparency, and sustainability.

Our Mission

  • Empower local farmers with fair trade and capacity building.
  • Ensure product quality that meets international export standards.
  • Maintain full traceability and proper export documentation.
  • Grow sustainably while protecting local ecosystems.

Quality Assurance

We maintain strict quality control at every step — from harvest to export.

Seaweed — Quality Process

Harvesting

Direct sourcing from trusted coastal farmers with sustainable practices.

Cleaning & Drying

Careful cleaning and controlled drying to ensure ideal moisture content.

Inspection & Export

Lab testing, COA, phytosanitary checks and packing for export compliance.

Spices — Quality Process

Selection

Only mature cloves and pepper are selected to preserve high quality.

Sun Drying & Sorting

Natural drying and manual sorting deliver consistent quality and color.

QC & Packing

Testing for purity and final packaging with moisture barrier and labeling.

Coffee — Quality Process

Selective Harvest

Handpicked cherries from partnered smallholder farms for consistency.

Processing & Roasting

Controlled processing and roasting to achieve desired flavor profiles.

Quality Check & Packaging

Moisture check, grading and vacuum packaging for export freshness.
